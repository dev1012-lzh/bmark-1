Frequently asked questions

1. Error Code: 1001_FPS_TOO_LOW or 1001_FPS_LOWER_THAN_EXPECTED

This error occurs when your device's graphics performance is insufficient to maintain frame rates (10FPS) in testing phase. Consider closing background applications, switching to a dGPU instead of using iGPU or updating your graphics drivers.

2. Screen shivers when benchmarking.

This happens when the FPS drop.
